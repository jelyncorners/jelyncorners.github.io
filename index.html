About Me

I worked as a freelance web developer online while enjoying nature, and learning different cultures, traditions, languages, food, and people. I worked to be flexible. At times I was asked to do graphic design and I love bringing my creative side to these requests. At other times the requests required full stack development starting with a database backend, a middle tier, and front end code in Javascript, HTML, and CSS.
